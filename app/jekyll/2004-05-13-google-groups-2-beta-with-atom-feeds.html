Google Groups 2 BETA offers Atom feeds for each group. To see feeds for a specific group, use this format:

https://groups-beta.google.com/group/NAME-OF-GROUP/feeds

Here are a few examples:

https://groups-beta.google.com/group/alt.www.webmaster/feeds
https://groups-beta.google.com/group/alt.html/feeds
